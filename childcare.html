Two babies both girls, Cara and Quinn. Born 1 May 2021.

Feeding

  • Usually when they wake up we feed them 6oz of formula w/ 1 mL of Poly-Vi-Sol
  • Feedings afterwards are 5 oz
  • Usually we feed them X times for 6 + (X-1) * 5 = Y oz over the course of the whole day
  • We also feed them solids foods in the evenings and sometimes during the day
    • For a list of solids they've eaten go to the bottom of this page
  • Baby Brezza
  • We make all the formula using the baby brezza, it's easy except when it doesn't work :)
  • Using the Baby Brezza
    • There are two bottons you press
    • The first is the "ounce" button, use this to change how much the baby brezza makes
    • The second button is the "start" button, this makes the formula. The process takes a bit longer than you might expect
  • When the baby brezza doesn't work
    • There three potential poblems when the baby brezza won't make formula
    • The first is that there may not be any formula powder, you can see how much formula is loaded in the hopper easily
    • The second problem this that the baby brezza is out of water, you see a water symbol with a line through it when this happens.
    • The last problem is that the baby brezza needs to be "cleaned," it needs to be cleaned after you've pressed the start button four times (two feedings for twins)

Sleeping

  • Cara and Quinn typically wake up at 6:30AM but they can wake up as late as 7:30AM
    • They usually sleep through the night without any problems
  • We try to nap them 2-3 times a day depending on how long their naps are
    • The shortest naps they should take are 30 minutes, please don't let them nap longer than 2 hours
    • Don't let them stay awake longer than 3 hours
